About the Project

This project offers a sustainable alternative to traditional real estate practices by utilizing a unique time-share model. Our goal is to democratize land ownership, making it accessible to a wider community, and ultimately return the land to its original indigenous inhabitants.

Project Details

  • Time-Share Model: 365 shares per housing unit, each representing one day.
  • Pricing: $250 per day-share.
  • Total Cost: $570,000 per unit (including land and infrastructure).
  • Goal: Raise sufficient funds through crowdfunding to cover costs and return the land to indigenous communities after 50 years.

How to Participate

Invest in a share of a housing unit by purchasing one or more days. Your investment helps make this vision a reality and supports the sustainable development of community-owned land.
